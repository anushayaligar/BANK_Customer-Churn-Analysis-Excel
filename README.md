📊 Customer Churn Analysis (Excel Project)
🔹 Objective

To analyze customer churn and identify key factors affecting customer retention.

🔹 Tools Used
Excel (Pivot Tables, XLOOKUP, Dashboard)


🔹 Key Insights
Senior customers show highest churn (~45%)
Customers with 2 products are most stable (~8%)
Customers with 3–4 products show very high churn (83%–100%)
Customer experience impacts churn more than product count
